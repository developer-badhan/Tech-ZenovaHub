Help & Support

Tech‑ZenovaHub is a dynamic tech-oriented platform designed to connect customers and staff for seamless service interaction. It allows users to manage profiles, view dashboards based on their role, and perform specific actions in a secure, modern environment.

  • 🔑 Login: Click "Login" from the navbar and enter your credentials.
  • 📝 Sign Up: New here? Register using the "Sign up" button to create your account.
  • 🙋 Profile: Access your profile from the dropdown under your name once logged in.
  • 🚪 Logout: Use the Sign Out option in the user menu to safely log out.

Depending on your role:
  • Customer Dashboard: View orders, status updates, and support tickets.
  • Staff Dashboard: Manage customers, respond to queries, and update progress.

  • 🎨 Accent Color: Use the palette icon in the navbar to change the site’s accent color instantly.
  • 🌙 Theme Mode: Switch between light and dark mode using the moon/sun icon in the navbar.

  • Login Error: Ensure your username/password is correct and that your account is active.
  • Dashboard Not Loading: Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache.
  • Accent/Theme not saving: Preferences are saved temporarily. Persistent settings may require future updates.

Need more help? Reach out to our support team!
