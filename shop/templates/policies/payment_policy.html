Payment Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Accepted Payment Methods

We accept all major debit/credit cards, UPI, net banking, and popular digital wallets. All payments are processed through secure and encrypted gateways.

2. Payment Confirmation

Once your payment is successfully processed, you will receive an email confirmation with your order details. Orders are not dispatched until payment is verified.

3. Currency and Pricing

All prices are listed in INR (₹) and inclusive of applicable taxes unless stated otherwise. Prices may change without prior notice based on market conditions or promotions.

4. Failed Transactions

If your payment fails, please verify your bank details or try an alternative method. If funds are deducted without order confirmation, they will be automatically refunded within 5–7 business days.

5. Fraud Prevention

For security reasons, Tech-ZenovaHub reserves the right to verify transactions and may cancel suspicious orders without prior notice.

6. Contact Us

If you face any payment-related issues, please contact support@techzenovahub.com.
